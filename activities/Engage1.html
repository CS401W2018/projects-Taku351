What are special entity characters?

Special entity characters are characters that have special maning in HTML, such as & and <

What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags are special tags that used to mark specific parts of text within a document giving them semantic meaning and indicating their importance or context.

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

An OL is used when the order of items in a list matters, while a UL is used when the order of items does not matter and they are typically displayed with bullet points.
