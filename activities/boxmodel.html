Engagement questions

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take up the full width and start on a new line, while inline tags only take up as much space as needed and stay on the same line.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the space outside, padding is the space inside, and border is the line around an element.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

In CSS, we can use units like pixels (px), percentages (%), em, rem, viewport (vw, vh), and physical units (cm, mm, in).

When would you use a figure tag?

We use the figure tag to group images, videos, or charts with their descriptions (captions).
