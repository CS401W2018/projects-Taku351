What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is best for photos with smooth colors, but not for images with sharp edges or transparency.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIF is best for simple images, logos, and animations with few colors.

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG is unique because it keeps image quality high and supports transparent backgrounds.
