Title and Topic

Title: Journey & Frame: A Traveler’s Marketplace

Topic: This website will serve as both a travel blog and a place to buy and sell photos. I plan to show my own travel pictures (many in unique frames) and offer a space for people to request photography services. There will be a survey for feedback, a marketplace with a live chat for travelers and remote workers, plus a page for advertisements.

Why I Chose This Topic

I’ve always loved visiting new places and taking pictures. By making a site that features my photographs and also allows others to share or request photos, I can build a community around travel and creativity. It also addresses the challenge of finding local photographers when traveling.

Page Breakdown

  • Home Page:
    • Intro to the site and a short welcome note
    • A few featured travel photos
    • Links to the main pages
    • A brief “About Me” section
  • Page 1: Travel Journey
    • Blog-style travel posts
    • Photo galleries with captions and locations
    • A map showing places I’ve visited
  • Page 2: Photo Shop & Requests
    • Photo Shop:
      • Gallery of photos for sale
      • Details about frames or cases
      • Purchase options with pricing
    • Photography Requests:
      • A form for users to describe their photography needs (e.g., events, portraits)
      • Info on how I (or other photographers) can respond with quotes and availability
  • Page 3: Customer Survey (with a Table)
    • A survey form to collect feedback about photo quality or service
    • Fields: Name, Email, Photo/Service, Ratings (1-5), Comments
    • A table to show submitted feedback (ID, Name, Email, Ratings, Comments)
  • Page 4: Community Marketplace & Chat
    • Marketplace:
      • Users can list their own travel photos for sale
      • Registration/login for sellers
      • Guidelines for posting and contacting sellers
    • Chat:
      • Real-time chat for travelers and remote workers to share tips
      • Basic moderation to keep conversations respectful
      • Possible archives for past chats
  • Page 5: Advertisements & Promotions
    • Space for travel or photography-related ads
    • Promotions, deals, or sponsored content
    • Upcoming travel expos or events

Form and Table Details

  • Survey/Request Forms:
    • Name, Email
    • Photo Purchased / Photography Request (optional)
    • Quality/Service Rating (1-5)
    • Comments (open text)
    • Request Details (type of session, preferred dates, etc.)
  • Survey Results Table:
    • Submission ID (unique)
    • Name, Email
    • Ratings
    • Comments
